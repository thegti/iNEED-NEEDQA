about us

incorporated in qatar, need.qa is an online business to business market place that connects buyers and sellers. this portal focuses on providing a platform to small and medium enterprises (smes), large enterprises and individuals to increase their sales leads and grow their supplier network.

need.qa for buyers (customers)

  • convenience of sending a requirement to “n” number of suppliers in one click. a requirement posted by a customer in need.qa is shared with its registered vendors.
  • access to local and international suppliers in one platform
  • no brokerage or commissions to be paid
  • no registrations; it’s absolutely free to reach your supplier network.

need.qa for sellers (suppliers)

  • enhanced business coverage for your products and services
  • increased number of sales leads and connect with more customers
  • no brokerage or commissions to be paid
  • minimal monthly or annual subscription fees
  • just upload your ‘search key words’ to need.qa and start getting the sales leads.

grow your sales & supplier network with key words!

let the key words, bring in business for you.

it’s simple! key words are those words which customers often search for or look for your products or services. upload your business key words to need.qa; and just start getting business / sales leads.

how it works!

step 1: customer selects auto populated key word and enters their requirement in need.qa

step 2: need.qa will share it with its registered vendors to provide the quotation for the customer : customer gets responses with in 2 working days from the supplier

step 3: customer gets responses with in 2-3 working days from the supplier

step 4: customer evaluates the responses and closes the deal

free trial period – 7 days.
